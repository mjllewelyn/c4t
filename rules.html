Rules - Connect 4 Takeover

Back to Game

Objective

As the name suggests the objective of the game is to get 4 in a row.

The Catch

There are different pieces that can take your opponent's pieces.

On Your Turn

On your turn you will select a piece and then select where you want to put it. When it is your turn the color behind your pieces will show your color.

The Pieces

Circle - You get 4 circles, but they cannot take anything and can be taken by anything except another circle

Triangle - You get 3 triangles, and they can take circles but can be taken by rectangles and squares

Rectangle - You get 2 rectangles, and they can take circles and triangles but can be taken by a square

Square - You only get 1 square, which can't be taken and it can take anything except another square
